Prompts that actually work

🧠 Reasoning

Step-by-Step Reasoner

Forces Claude to reason through a problem before giving an answer — dramatically improves accuracy on complex tasks.

Before answering, think through this step by step. Show your reasoning in a <thinking> block, then give your final answer clearly labeled as <answer>.

[Your question here]
✍️ Writing

Voice Mimic

Give Claude samples of your writing and it will match your style perfectly — tone, rhythm, vocabulary, everything.

Here are three examples of my writing:

[SAMPLE 1]
[SAMPLE 2]
[SAMPLE 3]

Now write [TASK] in exactly my voice. Match my sentence length, vocabulary level, and tone. Do not add filler or make it more formal.
💻 Code

Code Reviewer

Ruthless but constructive code review. Catches bugs, style issues, and design problems you'd miss yourself.

Review this code as a senior engineer would. Focus on:
1. Bugs or edge cases that could cause failures
2. Security vulnerabilities
3. Performance issues
4. Readability and maintainability

Be direct. Don't praise things that are merely acceptable. Suggest concrete fixes.

```
[YOUR CODE]
```
🔍 Research

Devil's Advocate

Force Claude to steelman the opposing view before presenting its own. Eliminates confirmation bias from AI-assisted research.

I'm going to share my position on [TOPIC]. Your job:

1. First, argue AGAINST my position as convincingly as possible — find the strongest counterarguments
2. Then identify which of my assumptions are weakest
3. Finally, give me your honest synthesis

My position: [YOUR POSITION]
⚡ Productivity

Socratic Clarifier

Before tackling any complex task, have Claude ask all the questions it needs. Prevents wasted effort on misunderstood tasks.

Before you attempt this task, ask me every clarifying question you need to do it perfectly. Don't start the actual task until I've answered. List all questions at once — don't ask one at a time.

Task: [YOUR TASK]
✂️ Writing

Brutal Editor

Cut the fat from your writing. Claude acts as a ruthless editor focused on clarity and impact — no sugarcoating.

Edit this text for maximum clarity and impact. Rules:
- Cut every word that doesn't earn its place
- Replace weak verbs with strong ones
- Eliminate all filler phrases ("it's worth noting that", "in order to", etc.)
- Keep my voice — don't rewrite, just sharpen
- Show me the edited version, then a list of every change you made and why

[YOUR TEXT]
🎯 Reasoning

Decision Framework

Structure ambiguous decisions with explicit tradeoffs. Great for career choices, product decisions, or any high-stakes call.

Help me think through this decision.

Decision: [DECISION]
Options: [LIST OPTIONS]
My main goal: [WHAT MATTERS MOST]
Constraints: [TIME/MONEY/OTHER LIMITS]

Build a decision matrix. Weight each criterion by importance to me. Score each option honestly. Then give me your actual recommendation — don't hedge.
🔧 Code

Rubber Duck Debugger

Describe your bug and have Claude systematically walk through every possible cause — often catches what you've been blind to.

I have a bug. Walk me through it systematically.

Expected behavior: [WHAT SHOULD HAPPEN]
Actual behavior: [WHAT'S HAPPENING]
What I've already tried: [YOUR ATTEMPTS]

Code:
```
[YOUR CODE]
```

Hypothesize every possible root cause, ranked by likelihood. For each one, tell me exactly how to verify or rule it out.
📋 Productivity

Persona Injector

Assign Claude a specific expert persona for your task. Unlocks domain-specific reasoning patterns and vocabulary.

You are [EXPERT TYPE — e.g. "a principal engineer at a FAANG company with 15 years of distributed systems experience"]. You think and communicate like this person — you use their vocabulary, make the tradeoffs they would make, and have their priorities.

With that lens: [YOUR QUESTION OR TASK]
